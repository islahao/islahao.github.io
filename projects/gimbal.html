3 Axis Gimbal

Abstract

Gimbals are mechanically fascinating devices, used among industry professionals to hobbyists. In photography and filmography, gimbals effectively offset any unwanted motion or jitters on the camera, creating the "buttery smooth" effect. Today, many handheld gimbals for phones and cameras can reach up to hundreds of £s, due to its high material and technology requirements. Industry leaders such as DJI offer flagship smartphone gimbals for around £100.
